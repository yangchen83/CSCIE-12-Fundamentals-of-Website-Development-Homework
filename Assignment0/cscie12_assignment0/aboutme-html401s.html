What do I do?

I'm a postdoc at the Johns Hopkins University Bloomberg School of Public Health.

I'm working in Dr.Dimopoulos' lab. The main goal of our lab is to understand the immune system of mosquitoes, which are vectors of diseases such as malaria and dengue fever. The idea is that if we can help mosquitoes fight against pathogens, they would not be able to transmit the diseases to human beings.

I'm currently working on characterizing an important molecule in the mosquito immune system, and I'm also trying to generate transgenic mosquitoes that can resist malaria parasite infection.
